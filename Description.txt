## 🔍 Bias Analysis

### Educational Background:
- **A-tier candidates:** 2 (IIT Madras, RV College)
- **B-tier candidates:** 2 (SSN College, PES University)
- **C-tier candidates:** 3 (BMS College, Christ University, Polytechnic)
- **D-tier candidates:** 3 (COEP, CBIT, Amity)

**Finding:** AI evaluated skills first, then applied Passport Score weighting. Self-taught candidate (Meera Pillai) ranked above fresh graduates despite D-tier education.

### Experience Level:
- **0-2 years:** 4 candidates
- **3-5 years:** 4 candidates
- **6-8 years:** 2 candidates

**Finding:** Experience alone didn't determine rank. Skills match and context mattered more (e.g., Rohit with 4 years backend-only ranked #8).
